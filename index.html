SNOW Whitening Toothpaste

SNOW Whitening Toothpaste is a brand which aimed to target a younger audience after successfully attracting older clients in previous campaigns. In this advertisement, I aimed to keep the copy simple but informative. Additionally, I wanted the pique the viewer's interest with the question: “What does yours say?”

GameStop: Assassin's Creed Spec Ad

In the original GameStop ad, attention is drawn to the fact that the game is incomplete. Consumers are discouraged from pre-ordering, instead of called to do so. I modified the copy to challenge the audience to the bonus mission, instead of reminding them that it is exclusive content.

Monocle Media

Monocle Media specializes in capturing essential moments of the human experience. This includes weddings, babies, events, and fictional pieces. Monocle Media prides itself on keeping a low profile on set, while also developing personal relationships with their customers. I aimed to craft a tagline to embody that.
